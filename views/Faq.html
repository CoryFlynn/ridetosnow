Is riding with Ski Lift safe?

Absolutely. Ski Lift screens all drivers, accepting only those who are at least
18 years of age and have a valid license and vehicle insurance.

Selecting a driver

You can see all ride options available in your area before confirming your ride.
Usually, several drivers will be available to get you to the slopes safely and comfortably.

Requesting more than one vehicle

You may contact as many drivers as you want to set up rides for your group.
Keep in mind that some rides can hold more than one person.

Riding With Pets

Please let your Ski Lift driver know if you will be travelling with any pets.

Lost/Forgotten Items

We advise sharing contact info with drivers in case of forgotton belongings.
If an item needs to be retrieved, please contact your driver.
